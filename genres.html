My Favorite Movies

Here are some of my favorite movies that I think you'll enjoy too! Click on the cards to read more about my reviews for each movie.

The Dark Knight Rises

"The Dark Knight Rises" captivates with its epic scope and emotional depth, providing a fitting and powerful conclusion to Christopher Nolan's Batman trilogy. Bale's nuanced portrayal of Bruce Wayne, coupled with Hardy's menacing Bane, delivers a complex duel of ideology and will that anchors the film's thrilling narrative and spectacular action.

Inception

"Inception" is a wild ride into dream-stealing adventures with Leonardo DiCaprio at the helm. The visuals are mind-blowing, and the plot twists will have you second-guessing reality. It's a smart, thrilling movie that packs in action, emotion, and some seriously cool ideas about dreams. Definitely a must-watch that keeps you thinking long after it ends.

Ameilie

Just finished "Amélie," and wow, what a ride! The movie's charm is off the charts, with Amélie's quirky ways of helping others bringing so much warmth. Paris is basically another character here, full of color and life. It's one of those movies that makes you want to be a better person.

Forest Gump

It is an emotional rollercoaster. Tom Hanks is phenomenal as Forrest, and the way he stumbles through historic moments is both hilarious and moving. It's got love, war, friendship, and shrimps - what more could you want? This movie makes you laugh, cry, and think about life's unpredictability.

The Intouchables

I couldn't stop laughing and crying. It's about this rich guy who's paralyzed and his caregiver from the projects, and it's just beautiful how they bond and change each other's lives. Its funny without trying too hard, and the friendship feels real. Totally warms your heart and makes you believe in humanity again.

Finding Nemo

It is an adventure that takes you under the sea and into the heart. The animation is breathtaking, and the story of Marlin looking for his son, Nemo, hits right in the feels. Dory is hilarious, and the dangers of the ocean have never looked so inviting. Its a story about bravery and love that is perfect for any age.

Up

Up starts with a punch to the gut in the best possible way, telling a love story in minutes. Then it turns into this wild adventure of an old man, a boy, and a talking dog flying to South America in a house lifted by balloons. Its about finding adventure and friendship at any age. It makes you want to chase your dreams, no matter how crazy they seem.

Little Miss Sunshine

It's brutally honest and hilariously sad at the same time. A family full of misfits takes a road trip to a beauty pageant, and everything that can go wrong, does. But it's their flaws that make you love them even more. It's a weird, wild ride that ends up being super uplifting.

The Princess Bride

It's a perfect mix of romance, adventure, and comedy with some of the most quotable lines in movie history.Whether it's the swashbuckling Westley, the formidable Inigo Montoya, or the hilarious Vizzini, there's someone for everyone to love. It's the kind of movie that makes you feel like a kid again, full of wonder and excitement.

Good Will Hunting

Saw "Good Will Hunting" again and it still hits hard. Its amazing how a movie about a math genius working as a janitor can be so touching. Matt Damon and Robin Williams are incredible together, and the script is smart and emotional. It’s a story about finding your place in the world that leaves you feeling inspired.

Sing Street

"Sing Street" took me by surprise. Itss set in the '80s and is all about this kid forming a band to impress a girl, but its so much more. The music is amazing, and the story is a heartfelt nod to anyone who is ever dreamed big. It's a feel-good movie that actually feels good, reminding you of the power of dreams and music.

Julie & Julia

It is a delightful watch. Meryl Streep as Julia Child is perfection, and Amy Adams’ journey as a blogger cooking her way through Julia's recipes is so relatable. It is a movie about passion, food, and finding what makes you happy. Made me want to cook, and I dont even like cooking.

Chef

Its about a chef starting a food truck and reconnecting with his son. The dishes look amazing, and the father-son dynamic is really sweet. Its a road trip movie that makes you want to eat your way across the country.

Paddington

The most heartwarming movie ever. A bear from Peru in London getting into all sorts of trouble but with the best intentions? It’s as cute as it sounds. The humor is great for both kids and adults, and the message about family and acceptance is beautiful.

About Time

Just got around to watching "About Time" and honestly, it was more touching than I expected. The time travel aspect is a neat twist, but it's really the emotional depth and relatable moments that make it shine. The film beautifully mixes humor with lifes tougher lessons about love and loss. Really pulls at the heartstrings while making you appreciate the little things in life.
